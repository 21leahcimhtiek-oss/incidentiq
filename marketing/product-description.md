# IncidentIQ — Product Description

## Tagline
AI incident management. Zero manual post-mortems.

## Elevator Pitch
IncidentIQ is the incident management platform that uses AI to do the work your team hates. When something breaks at 2am, IncidentIQ automatically classifies the severity, pages the right on-call engineer, tracks every action in real-time, and generates a complete post-mortem when the dust settles — all without anyone writing a single word.

## Problem We Solve
Modern engineering teams spend 40% of incident resolution time on coordination and documentation. Post-mortems get skipped because they take 2-3 hours to write. On-call engineers waste critical minutes triaging severity instead of fixing the problem. Teams have no visibility into trends until it's too late.

## Solution
IncidentIQ automates the entire incident lifecycle:

1. **Automatic Severity Classification**: Submit an incident with title and description. GPT-4 instantly classifies it as P0-P3, so your team knows the blast radius before reading the ticket.

2. **Smart On-Call Routing**: The right engineer gets paged immediately for P0/P1 incidents. Rotation schedules are tracked. No more "who's on-call?" Slack threads.

3. **Real-Time Timeline**: Every update, status change, and comment is timestamped and ordered. Everyone sees the same story, live.

4. **AI Post-Mortems**: When an incident is resolved, one click generates a comprehensive post-mortem from the full timeline — complete with root cause analysis, action items, and MTTR metrics. What used to take 3 hours takes 3 seconds.

5. **MTTR Analytics**: Track mean time to resolve by severity and team. Spot trends. Prevent the next incident.

## Who It's For
- **SRE teams** who want to reduce manual toil during and after incidents
- **DevOps teams** who need structured incident management without heavyweight enterprise tools
- **Engineering managers** who want visibility into incident trends without bothering their team

## Competitive Advantages
- AI-native: Built with OpenAI from day one, not bolted on
- Developer-friendly REST API for alert ingestion from any monitoring tool
- Simple pricing without per-seat complexity
- Full post-mortem automation — not just templates

## Key Metrics We Improve
- Time to classify severity: Hours -> Seconds
- Time to write post-mortem: 3 hours -> 3 seconds
- Mean time to acknowledge: Reduce by 60% via automatic on-call routing
- Post-mortem completion rate: 20% -> 100% (because it's automatic)
