## Value Propositions

### ROI Calculation

For a 10-person engineering team:
- Average incident duration: 2 hours
- Post-mortem writing time: 2-3 hours
- Incidents per month: 20
- Engineer hourly cost: $75/hour

**With IncidentIQ:**
- Severity classification: saves 15 min/incident = $375/month
- Post-mortem automation: saves 2.5 hours/incident x 20 = $3,750/month
- Total savings: ~$4,125/month
- IncidentIQ Pro cost: $149/month
- **ROI: 27x**

### Key Differentiators

1. **AI-native, not AI-bolted-on**: Built with OpenAI from day one. Severity classification and post-mortem generation are core features, not add-ons.

2. **Zero-friction post-mortems**: Competitors offer templates. We generate complete post-mortems from incident timelines. No staring at a blank document.

3. **Simple REST API for alerts**: Any monitoring tool can push alerts in seconds. No agents, no plugins, no complex integrations.

4. **Transparent pricing**: No per-seat complexity. Flat monthly pricing that scales with incidents, not headcount (Starter) or unlimited (Pro).

5. **Full-stack open source**: MIT licensed. Run it yourself or use our hosted version.

## Marketplace Listing Copy

**Short description (160 chars):**
AI-powered incident management with automatic post-mortems. Classify severity, route on-call, track timelines, generate post-mortems in one click.

**Long description:**
IncidentIQ eliminates the most painful parts of incident management for engineering teams.

When an incident hits, your team should be focused on fixing it — not triaging severity, figuring out who to page, or worrying about writing the post-mortem later.

IncidentIQ automates all of that:

**AI Severity Classification**: Submit an incident. Get P0-P3 back in milliseconds using GPT-4. No more 10-minute triage discussions during a P0.

**Smart On-Call Routing**: IncidentIQ knows who's on-call right now and pages them automatically via Slack for P0 and P1 incidents.

**Real-Time Timeline**: Every update, status change, and note is captured in order. Your entire team sees the same story, live.

**One-Click Post-Mortems**: When the incident is resolved, click "Generate Post-Mortem." AI reads the full timeline and produces a complete document — executive summary, root cause analysis, action items, MTTR metrics — in seconds.

**MTTR Analytics**: Dashboard showing mean time to resolve by severity and team. Spot patterns. Prevent future incidents.

**Alert Ingestion API**: Push alerts from Datadog, PagerDuty, Grafana, or any custom tool via a simple REST API.

Built for teams who want the discipline of structured incident management without the overhead of enterprise tools.
